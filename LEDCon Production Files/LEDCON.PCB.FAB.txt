LEDCON.PCB.v1.0

- Material: FR4
- Thickness: 0.062"
- All vias tented (included in soldermask layers)
- Solder mask: BLACK
- Silk screen: WHITE
- Hole diameters applicable after plating
- UL796 Flammability Rating 94V-0. Fabricator's UL Logo shall be placed on bottom side of board
- Date of manufacture and lot code shall be placed on bottom side of board
- Stackup: 
  Top Paste         - LEDCON.PCB.SPT
  Top Silkscreen    - LEDCON.PCB.SST 
  Top Soldermask    - LEDCON.PCB.SMT
  Top Copper        - LEDCON.PCB.LAY1
  Bottom Copper     - LEDCON.PCB.LAY4
  Bottom Soldermask - LEDCON.PCB.SMB
  Bottom Silkscreen - LEDCON.PCB.SSB
  Bottom Paste      - LEDCON.PCB.SPB
  Excellon Drill    - LEDCON.PCB.drd
  Board Outline     - LEDCON.PCB.FAB
  Component centroid- LEDCON.PCB_centroid.csv
